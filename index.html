Equipment

Equipment needed to get started

  • 1 gallon demijohn
  • funnel to fit the demijohn (around 18cm)
  • hydrometer
  • small measuring cylinder
  • siphon with tap
  • bubbler airlock
  • bottle capper
  • bored cork bung
  • food thermometer with probe
  • large pipette or baster
  • polyester brew bag (such as a Vinclasse small straining bag)
  • room thermometer
  • scales (accurate to a tenth of a gram, but you'll also need normal kitchen scales to measure up to around 600g)
  • a reasonably big cooking pot
  • a bucket

Ingredients and equipment needed for each brew

  • hops (pellet or leaf)
  • cereals such as roasted barley, crystal malt
  • dry malt extract
  • brewing sugar
  • brewing yeast
  • bottle caps
  • 7 500 ml brown beer bottles
  • sterilizer such as bruclens

The total cost of the equipment will come to around £60, and the ingredients should come to around £5 per brew.
